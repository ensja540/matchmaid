# Match Maid — Launch Marketing Kit

Ready-to-post copy and a distribution playbook for getting Match Maid picked up on
Google, AI answer engines, Facebook, Reddit, Instagram, TikTok, and physical
community boards.

**The golden rule (marketplace cold-start):** recruit *cleaners* first, in a few
adjoining Christchurch suburbs, until a customer search returns 3+ real cleaners.
Only then push hard on the household side. Density beats spread.

URL to share everywhere: **matchmaid.co.nz**

---

## 0. Google + AI (already built — you just need to switch it on)

The site now ships `robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`, `llms.txt`, and JSON-LD structured
data. To activate discovery:

1. **Google Search Console** — add `matchmaid.co.nz` as a property, verify (DNS TXT
   or the HTML tag), then **Sitemaps → submit `https://matchmaid.co.nz/sitemap.xml`.**
2. **Bing Webmaster Tools** — same thing (Bing also feeds ChatGPT/Copilot search).
3. **Google Business Profile** — even as a directory, create one for "Match Maid"
   (service-area business, Christchurch). This is how you show up in Maps + the
   local pack for "cleaner near me".
4. Re-share the homepage link on any channel — that's what makes crawlers re-visit.

AI answer engines (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini) read `llms.txt` and the
structured data. The more you're mentioned on Reddit/FB/press, the more likely they
are to cite you — so the social posts below *also* feed AI discovery.

## 3. Reddit (r/chch, r/christchurch, later r/auckland)

Reddit *hates* ads. Lead with value, disclose that it's yours, don't spam. One
honest post beats ten promo drops.

**Title:** Made a free, no-commission directory for Christchurch cleaners — feedback welcome

> Kia ora — I got sick of the "can anyone recommend a cleaner?" posts going nowhere,
> so I built **matchmaid.co.nz**: a local directory where you can see a cleaner's
> rate, reviews and verified badges up front, then contact them directly. It's free
> for households, and cleaners keep 100% (no commission).
>
> It's early and I'm building supply in Christchurch first. Full disclosure, it's my
> project — I'm posting because I'd genuinely like feedback from locals, and because
> if you're a cleaner, listing is free right now. Happy to answer anything.

**Rules:** check each subreddit's self-promo policy; some need mod approval. Engage
in comments, don't drive-by. Never post the same text to many subs at once.

## 5. TikTok (same handle)

Short, native, no hard sell. Hooks that work:

1. **Hook:** "POV: you need a cleaner but refuse to ask Facebook again."
   → screen-record browsing matchmaid.co.nz, land on a profile with rate + reviews.
   Caption: "Rates up front. Free for you. Christchurch 🧽 matchmaid.co.nz"
2. **Hook:** "Cleaners in NZ — stop giving away 20% commission."
   → talk to camera: keep 100%, set your own rates, free to list right now.
3. **Hook:** "3 green flags to look for before you hire a house cleaner" —
   #3 = verified badges (ID, criminal check, insured), tie back to the site.

Post 3–5x/week; volume + a good hook beats polish. Reuse the IG reels here.

## 6. Community boards (supermarkets, cafés, libraries, laundromats)

Print `marketing/flyer.html` (A5, tear-off tabs with the URL). Put them up in the
target suburbs — New World / PAK'nSAVE noticeboards, cafés, community centres,
libraries, laundromats, vet clinics. Two versions of the pitch:

- **Household boards:** "Need a cleaner? See rates up front, free — matchmaid.co.nz"
- **Cleaner-facing spots** (cleaning-supply shops, hardware stores): "Cleaners — free
  local leads, no commission. List at matchmaid.co.nz/for-maids"

## Cadence (first 4 weeks)

| Week | Focus | Actions |
|------|-------|---------|
| 1 | Supply | DM cleaners on Marketplace/FB; post in cleaning groups; put up cleaner flyers. Set up Search Console + submit sitemap. |
| 2 | Supply | Keep recruiting until 8–10 live cleaners in 3 suburbs. Google Business Profile. First IG/TikTok posts. |
| 3 | Demand | Household posts in suburb groups; Reddit value post; household flyers up. |
| 4 | Both | Answer "recommend a cleaner?" threads; ask happy cleaners to refer peers (referral credit). Review what converts. |

**Don't** buy paid ads until a search in your target suburbs returns 3+ badged
cleaners. Ads on thin supply just burn money and goodwill.
